Treatment Highlight:
Stellate Ganglion Block

A stellate gangion block (SGB) injection is a local anesthetic injection and minimally invasive procedure used to treat a wide range of conditions, including pain, hot flashes, and sleep disturbances.

Treatment Highlight:
Ketamine IV Treatment

Intravenous (IV) Ketamine Infusions have been implemented in treatment plans for humans for over 50 years. By using Ketamine IV Infusions, we are able to provide significant relief to our patients.

Treatment Highlight:
Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation, TMS, is a safe and non-invasive treatment for Depression. It uses an MRI-strength magnet to stimulate under-active areas of the brain that control depression.
